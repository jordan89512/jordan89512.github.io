My Background

Front End

Front-end development has captured my interest due to its unique blend of creativity and problem-solving. Since embarking on my coding journey in 2019, I have been drawn to front-end development as a means of expressing myself through programming. I find it exhilarating to discover logical solutions to everyday problems using my skills in this field. The ability to craft visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces fascinates me, as it allows me to combine my artistic inclinations with my technical expertise. Moreover, I am driven by the fact that front-end development is in high demand, making it an exciting and rewarding skillset to possess. As I continue on the path of a Full Stack Software Engineer, I am eager to share the knowledge I have gained and continue to acquire in front-end development.

Back End

Back-end development has captivated my interest for different reasons. While front-end focuses on the visual aspect, I appreciate the intricacies and complexities involved in building the foundation and functionality of a website or application. Back-end development provides the backbone that powers the user experience, allowing for seamless interactions and efficient data management. The ability to tackle the technical aspects of an application is immensely satisfying to me. Back-end development also presents an opportunity to optimize performance and scalability, ensuring that the software can handle increasing demands. Overall, the combination of problem-solving, technical depth, and the ability to create efficient and reliable systems is what draws me to back-end development as an integral part of my journey as a Software Engineer.
